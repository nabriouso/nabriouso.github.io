Overview
ReIndev is a mod for Minecraft Beta 1.7.3 created by Silveros and DasJafss that intends to bring back the feelings of fun, fear, and discovery from old Minecraft. ReIndev adds new blocks, items, mobs, and plenty of other new and exciting features to bring back that long lost feeling of mystery. ReIndev acts as more of a "fork" of Minecraft, in that it does not use a mod loader.
New Features and Gameplay Additions
Potions
Potions have been added to game and work similarly to how potions were originally supposed to work in Minecraft, utilizing a cauldron instead of a brewing stand. Pour in some water, chuck in some random ingredients, and you're good to go!
Brewing recipes, while randomized, are always the same recipe no matter what. Regular items do not correspond to any effects and are entirely random as to what effects they can produce when combined with other ingredients. Special fish can be used as brewing ingredients to "weight" or guarantee certain effects. With the usage of special "charge" items, one can alter the type of potion created in the cauldron and create special potion types such as splash potions or the new "flash flasks" and bottled flames.
Gears
In ReIndev, redstone, does not exist. Circuitry and power is instead handled by gears which can be placed on walls and are powered by motor blocks. Gears function practically identical to redstone and most machines can be converted to use gears. ReIndev adds many new blocks to help condense and simplify circuitry. Some new blocks used in common gear circuitry include logic gates (AND, OR, NOT, XOR), relay blocks and wait blocks.
Cubic Chunks and Floating Islands
One of the most impressive features ReIndev adds. Cubic chunks are chunks that are, well, cubic! Chunks in vanilla Beta 1.7.3 are 16x128x16, but are 16^3 in ReIndev. We use this for added world depth and extra world height. The default world height in ReIndev is 2048 blocks! We use this added headroom for our floating islands found at around y600 in the Overworld.
The floating islands in ReIndev are certainly a site to be seen! Beautiful cherry tree forests, huge icy peaks, lush grassy fields filled with flowers galore. Atop these floating islands lives a new mob, the Wyvern, a small winged dragon that hunts at night. Wyverns will tolerate players so long as its during the daytime or one does not try to harm them. During fights, wyverns shoot elemental fireballs and like to swoop down and take bites out of their targets.
New Biomes and World Generation
ReIndev adds many new biomes. From desolate limestone wastelands and vast quicksand deserts, to giant ice spikes and dense tropical rainforests, ReIndev adds many new locations for you to explore.
World generation in ReIndev consists of various islands scattered across short stretches of water. Not happy with the island you're on? Make a boat and head in a random direction and you'll likely find something entirely different from where you started. Most biomes in ReIndev have trees to make sure that no matter where you are that you will always have an access to wood, even in deserts!
New Blocks and Smaller Features
ReIndev adds many new building blocks: basalt, stucco, limestone, colored planks, brimstone, and soul sandstone just to name a few! ReIndev also adds many utility and quality-of-life blocks and items, such as crates which act as a sort of "shulker box", the incinerator which converts organic materials into ash and coal, and the wrench which changes the direction or properties of a block.
Most tools in ReIndev have a special ability or quirk to them to help them stand out and give them a unique purpose or function, or to fill niches that would otherwise be covered by enchantments. Stone tools have a "fortune" effect on certain blocks, golden tools have a "silk touch" ability, diamond tools get faster the more you use them, and molten tools (a new tool set added by ReIndev) have a "smelt touch" and "fire aspect" ability that automatically smelts blocks and inflicts fire onto mobs.
ReIndev also adds some technical quality-of-life features over Beta 1.7.3 such as an improved multiplayer experience with less latency and bugs, single player commands and a creative mode with flight, a modern styled texture system, world types, and much more.

    Installation Instructions
    MultiMC / Prism Launcher Recommended
  • Create a new b1.7.3 instance
  • Select the newly created instance
  • Click "Edit Instance"
  • Click the "Versions" tab
  • Click "Replace minecraft.jar" and locate your ReIndev .jar file
    • Betacraft Automatic Installation
  • Click "Edit Instance"
  • Click "Mods repository"
  • Open the ReIndev section
  • Select the latest release, and then click "Load"
    • Betacraft Manual Installation
  • Locate your ".betacraft" folder (located in app data)
  • Drop your ReIndev .jar file into the "versions" folder
  • Reopen Betacraft if it's already opened
  • Click "Select version" and then locate ReIndev
